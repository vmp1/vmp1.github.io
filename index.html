I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Economics at Tulane University. I graduated from Universidad de Los Andes in Colombia with B.Sc./M.A. in Economics.

My research interests include economics of education, inequality, and poverty. Currently, I focus on two research branches. First, I study the patterns of school closures in America and how accountability policies affect them. Second, I study income inequality and poverty with an emphasis on developing economies. I use advanced econometrics methods and microsimulations in my research.
